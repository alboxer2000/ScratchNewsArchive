## Wondering about the new “My Stuff” page?

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/c008aaea4246e8842dd56e5682f24fb0/a241e4edce7281a6-83/s75x75_c1/efbd7346faf48043aa29f9d66e61693aa96761a3.png)

My Stuff is where you keep track of your shared and unshared projects, as well as your studios. You can click the My Stuff button in the toolbar to get to your My Stuff page from anywhere else on the site. Any project you create will automatically be saved there - so it’s a little like your own personal hard drive built into the Scratch website.
