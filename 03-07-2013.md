## Updates to website and vector paint editor

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/84e7c2c96ad3e71a6f6ed4751eac3d70/2d720eaa6e3b9d68-45/s100x200/8356c93005ab5d6dc8ae5388667acf125f7f84bd.png)

We’ve just updated the beta site with a bunch of [improvements and bug fixes](http://beta.scratch.mit.edu/forums/topic/2898/). Work continues on the bitmap paint editor. We’re still planning on releasing it to the beta site for your feedback in the coming weeks. Meanwhile, we’re asking the community for help creating icons for the [Scratch News](http://beta.scratch.mit.edu/projects/10056467/) feed. There’ve been 63 submissions so far - thanks everyone!
