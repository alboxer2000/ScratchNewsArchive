## Reshaping drawings

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/18edc5ce9b17f06327b8c77a8db5efc2/9707f46a0b956c36-ce/s75x75_c1/11518ab5b40c4f392a785820888c00768131171e.png)

The reshape tool allows you to reshape any line or shape that you draw in the vector paint editor.

[This video](http://www.listenonrepeat.com/watch/?v=_CQiKkaq1Ec) shows Kimmy123 using the reshape tool to draw.

Tips:

- To reshape, click with the reshape tool

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/ea2ffd70da235660b2ccf55accaa6517/9707f46a0b956c36-2d/s75x75_c1/7d3ff4bd853e45ff88a8fb0c3d18c41ab31942a3.png)

, then drag any control point.

- To add a control point, click on a line. (To remove a control point, click it.)

- To turn a line into a closed shape, drag the end points so they meet. Then you can fill it with a color!

- To turn a closed shape into a line, using the reshape tool, hold Shift, then click a point.
